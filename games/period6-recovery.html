WHAT THIS GAME PRACTICES

      
This game is built for Period 6’s recovery group and mirrors the Period 4 recovery design. It drills Period 6’s biggest class trouble areas: First Amendment freedoms, federalism, Anti-Federalist versus Federalist arguments, formal versus informal constitutional change, fiscal versus monetary policy, and repeated core concept recognition until the ideas feel automatic.
