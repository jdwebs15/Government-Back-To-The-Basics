WHAT THIS GAME PRACTICES

      
Foundations, branches, checks and balances, federalism, Bill of Rights basics, Federalists vs. Anti-Federalists, and the difference between fiscal and monetary policy. Several ideas repeat on purpose so the low group gets maximum reps before OST.
